About This Project

Women's Health Tracker is a web-based platform designed to help women manage their health conditions by providing structured workout and diet plans. It focuses on conditions such as PCOS, PCOD, Fibroids, Thyroid Disorders, Breast Health Issues, Diabetes, and Weight Management.

How It Works

The platform offers a dedicated workout and diet plan for each health condition. Users can:

  • Navigate through different categories of health concerns.
  • Access structured workout and diet plans for each condition.
  • Mark their workouts as completed.
  • Track weekly progress through an interactive dashboard.

Features

  • Condition-specific workout plans.
  • Daily and weekly tracking of completed workouts.
  • Interactive dashboard displaying user statistics.
  • User-friendly interface with smooth navigation.

Developer

This project was developed by Arthi, a passionate developer dedicated to creating digital solutions that improve lives. The goal of this platform is to provide an easy-to-use health tracking system that supports women in their fitness and wellness journeys.
